Start Selling on Amazon

Why Sell on Amazon?

Reach millions of customers, streamline your business, and grow your brand by selling on Amazon. With Amazon's global reach and trusted marketplace, you can expand your sales online quickly and efficiently.

  • Access to millions of customers worldwide
  • Easy-to-use selling tools and resources
  • Flexible fulfillment options: Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) or Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM)
  • Receive payments directly to your bank account
  • Customer service handled by Amazon for FBA sellers

How to Start Selling

Starting your journey as a seller on Amazon is easy. Just follow these steps:

  1. Sign up for a seller account on Amazon Seller Central.
  2. Choose your selling plan: Individual or Professional.
  3. Add your products and list them on Amazon.
  4. Set your price and shipping options.
  5. Start managing your orders and grow your business!

Pricing Plans

Individual Plan

Perfect for occasional sellers.

Fees: $0.99 per item sold

Professional Plan

Ideal for regular or high-volume sellers.

Fees: $39.99 per month (no per-item fee)

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get paid?

    Payments are made to your bank account every 14 days, or faster if you opt for daily payments.

  • Can I sell internationally?

    Yes, you can sell to customers in many countries by choosing to list your products on Amazon's international marketplaces.

  • What are the fees to sell on Amazon?

    The fees depend on your selling plan. Individual sellers pay a per-item fee, while Professional sellers pay a monthly subscription fee.

  • Can Amazon help me with customer service?

    If you choose Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA), Amazon handles all customer service and returns for you.
